This project is a multi-page business website for "ImaginaryInns Hotel". The goal is to present the hotel in a visually appealing and user-friendly way, and to provide "visitors" with all essential information they need to learn about the hotel and make a booking.

The website consists of several interconnected pages:

Main Page

An overview of the hotel with a short introduction, key highlights, and quick access to the main sections of the site.

Rooms

A structured presentation of different room types, including short descriptions, key features, and prices.

Booking

A form where visitors can book room/rooms for their stay.

Contact

Contact details, address, and a contact form so visitors can reach the hotel with questions or special requests.

Services

Information about what typues of services the hotel offers to its guests.

Database
